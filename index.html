Episode XXII

In San Diego


It is a time of peace and prosperity in the Galaxy. Since Cool Stars 21, the James Webb Space Telescope has taken data, the Rubin Observatory Large Survey of Space and Time has seen first-light, and Euclid has launched. Major breakthroughts have happened in theory and computational astrophysics.

There is much to rejoice about, but something is brewing in San Diego. A small group of Stellar Astrophysicists have been planning something for 2024....
