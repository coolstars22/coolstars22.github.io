Episode XXII

IN SAN DIEGO


It is a time of scientific discovery throughout the Galaxy. Since Cool Stars 21, the James Webb Space Telescope has taken data, the Rubin Observatory Legacy Survey of Space and Time has seen first-light, and Euclid has launched.

Major breakthroughs have happened in theoretical and computational astrophysics. Now, with the ultimate scientific tools in hand, astrophysicists have enough power to unlock the secrets of the Universe.

There is much to rejoice about, but something is brewing in San Diego. A small group of astronomers have been planning something for 2024....
